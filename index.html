About Cali's Finest

At Cali's Finest, we specialize in importing the highest quality cannabis vapes directly from California's most reputable brands to the UK. Our mission is to bring authentic American cannabis experiences to discerning customers across the pond.

We work directly with licensed producers in California to ensure you receive genuine, lab-tested products exactly as they're meant to be experienced. From Packwoods to Backpack Boys, we carry only the most respected names in the industry.

Our team has years of experience in both the US and UK cannabis markets, ensuring smooth logistics and reliable delivery to your doorstep.

Payment Methods

Bitcoin Payments Only

For security and privacy, we only accept Bitcoin payments.

Once payment is confirmed on the blockchain (typically within 10-30 minutes), your order will be processed for shipment.
